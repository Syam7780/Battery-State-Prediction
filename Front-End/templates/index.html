Explainable Data-Driven Digital Twins for Predicting Battery States in Electric Vehicles

About Us

Our project develops Explainable Data-Driven Digital Twins (DTs) to predict the State of Charge (SoC) and State of Health (SoH) of electric vehicle batteries with high accuracy.

Using advanced machine learning and deep learning models, the system analyzes real-time battery data to provide reliable predictions and proactive insights.

To ensure transparency and trust, we integrate explainable AI (XAI) methods such as LIME and SHAP, which clearly illustrate the contribution of each feature to the model’s decisions.

This approach not only improves prediction performance but also empowers users and stakeholders with interpretable insights, enabling smarter battery management and sustainable electric mobility.
